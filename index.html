About Notes Portal

Notes Portal is a simple and efficient semester-wise platform designed to help students access and download academic notes for free. Whether you are studying Computer Science, Business, or Arts, we aim to make your learning journey smoother.

Especially for UOS Students: This portal is tailored to match the curriculum and syllabus of the University of Sargodha. All notes are organized semester-wise to help you find what you need quickly and efficiently.
